Why Therapy Is Important?

1. Mental Health Support: Provides professional help for managing stress, anxiety, and depression.
2. Self-Understanding: Helps individuals gain insight into their thoughts, emotions, and behaviors.
3. Coping Skills: Teaches effective strategies for handling life's challenges and difficult situations.
4. Conflict Resolution: Aids in resolving interpersonal issues and improving relationships.
5. Personal Growth: Encourages personal development and self-improvement.
